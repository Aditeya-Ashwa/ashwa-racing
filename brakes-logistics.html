01Role & Responsibility

The Brakes & Logistics subsystem ensures the vehicle stops reliably and logistics;car and team; are handled efficiently. We design, test and maintain hydraulic braking assemblies, parts of which are — calipers, discs, brake pads— while managing spares, workshops, transport, and race-day logistics.

Safety, repeatability and thermal performance are the primary engineering goals. The subsystem collaborates closely with Chassis, Electrical and Suspension subsystems to ensure braking integration and reliable data capture during development and racing.

02Core Areas

01

Mechanical Braking Design

Design and validate brake pedal, clutch pedal, pedal box and brake rotors using CAD and simulation.

02

Hydraulics & Control

Tuning the brake pressure by biasing the braking force to front and rear wheels

03

Testing & Validation

We ensure that brake rotors lock and are thermally and structurally stable, pedal assembly is designed upto driver ergonomics and ensure zero leaks in the brake lines

04

Logistics

We ensure that safe transportation of team, safe shipping of car and manage all sorts of documentation required for shipping of the car

03Vision & Roadmap

We aim to build braking systems that are predictable under load, light yet robust, supported by efficient logistics processes. Our roadmap emphasizes thermal and structural management, tighter integration with control systems and streamlined spare-parts flow.
