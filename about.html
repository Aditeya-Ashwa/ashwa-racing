About Us

Ashwa Racing, operating under the Ashwa Mobility Foundation, is the Formula Student team of R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), Bengaluru. Established in 2003, Ashwa stands as one of India’s premier collegiate motorsports teams, recognized for engineering precision, innovation, and consistent participation on the global Formula Student stage.

Our Mission & Vision

Our vision is to become a national leader across all Formula Student categories and drive sustainable mobility through student-led innovation.

Our mission is to provide real-world, industry-grade experience in mobility engineering, establish strong academic-industry partnerships, and build high-performance, eco-conscious prototypes.

History & Impact

With a legacy spanning over two decades, Ashwa Racing continues to push the boundaries of mobility engineering.

Having developed over 15 prototype race cars across combustion, hybrid, and electric powertrains, Ashwa remains committed to fostering young engineering talent and contributing to India’s automotive R&D landscape.
