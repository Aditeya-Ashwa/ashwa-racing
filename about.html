About Us

Ashwa Racing is the official Formula Student Hybrid team of RV College of Engineering. Founded in 2003, we design, manufacture, and race high-performance formula-style hybrid vehicles to compete internationally in Formula Bharat and Formula Hybrid events.

Our team is a multidisciplinary student organization with expertise across electrical, mechanical, electronics, aerodynamics, finance, management, and embedded systems.

With a legacy spanning over two decades, Ashwa Racing continues to pioneer engineering innovation, technical excellence, and motorsport passion in India.
