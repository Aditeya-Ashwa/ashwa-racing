About Us

Ashwa Racing, officially part of the Ashwa Racing, is the Formula Student team of R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), Bengaluru. Since its founding in 2003, Ashwa Racing has grown into one of India’s most respected student motorsport engineering teams, known for its technical rigor, innovation, and consistent international presence.

Our Mission & Vision

To be a national leader in all categories of Formula Student and to pioneer sustainable mobility solutions through student-led engineering innovation. Our mission is to equip students with real-world, industry-grade mobility engineering experience. Foster strong collaboration between academia and industry — partners and sponsors play a key role in our development. Challenge students to design and build high-performance, cost-effective, and eco-conscious prototype vehicles. Promote India’s engineering talent on a global stage by competing in international formula student events.

History & Impact

With a legacy spanning over two decades, Ashwa Racing continues to pioneer engineering innovation, technical excellence, and motorsport passion in India. Ashwa Racing is RVCE’s long-standing R&D platform. Since its inception in 2003, the team has developed over 15 prototype race cars, spanning combustion, hybrid, and electric architectures.
