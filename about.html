About Us

Ashwa Racing, operating under the Ashwa Mobility Foundation, is the Formula Student team of R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), Bengaluru. Since its establishment in 2003, the team has grown into one of India’s most respected student motorsport engineering groups, known for technical rigor, innovation, and consistent participation on the international Formula Student stage.

Our Mission & Vision

Our vision is to become a national leader across all Formula Student categories and to drive sustainable mobility through student-led engineering innovation.

Our mission is to provide students with real-world, industry-grade experience in mobility engineering, foster strong collaboration between academia and industry, design high-performance and eco-conscious prototype vehicles, and represent India's engineering talent on global Formula Student platforms.

History & Impact

With a legacy spanning more than two decades, Ashwa Racing continues to push the boundaries of engineering innovation, performance, and motorsport passion in India.

As RVCE’s long-standing automotive R&D platform, Ashwa Racing has developed over 15 prototype race cars across combustion, hybrid, and electric powertrains. The team remains committed to advancing sustainable mobility while nurturing the next generation of highly skilled engineers.
