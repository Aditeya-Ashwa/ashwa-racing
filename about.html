About Ashwa Racing

Ashwa Racing is the Formula Student team of R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), Bengaluru — engineering high-performance race cars through precision, innovation, and competition.

About Ashwa Racing

From inception to impact — our journey is one of innovation, growth, and engineering excellence.

Who We Are

Established in 2003 under the Ashwa Mobility Foundation, Ashwa Racing is one of India’s premier collegiate motorsports teams with consistent global Formula Student participation.

Mission & Vision

Our vision is to become a national leader across Formula Student categories and drive sustainable mobility through student-led innovation.

Our mission is to provide industry-grade engineering exposure, foster academic-industry collaboration, and develop high-performance prototypes.

History & Impact

Over two decades, Ashwa Racing has built more than 15 prototype race cars across combustion, hybrid, and electric powertrains — shaping engineers and contributing to India’s automotive R&D ecosystem.
