01What We Do

The Information Technology (IT) subsystem drives the digital innovation at Ashwa Racing. From designing and maintaining the official website to developing custom software for the team's operations, IT ensures seamless digital performance on and off the track.

Our responsibilities include developing and maintaining the team's custom ERP platform, handling Driverless simulation and machine learning systems, integrating data acquisition pipelines, and ensuring the technological ecosystem of Ashwa Racing remains scalable, secure, and efficient.

03Vision & Roadmap

Ashwa Racing's IT subsystem is built on a simple idea — to create a digital ecosystem that is fast, automated, scalable, and reliable. Our long-term vision focuses on enabling smarter engineering decisions, reducing manual effort across subsystems, and building tooling that accelerates innovation.
