Who We Are

Ashwa Racing is a Formula Student combustion team from RV College of Engineering, dedicated to designing, engineering, and manufacturing high-performance formula-style race cars. With over two decades of innovation, Ashwa remains one of India’s leading collegiate motorsports teams.

Learn More

Engineering Focus

Powertrain Engineering

Development of an optimized combustion engine system with custom intake, exhaust, and fuel delivery solutions designed for maximum efficiency and dynamic performance.

Aerodynamic Design

Full aerodynamic package development using CFD, including wings, undertray, and cooling ducts to improve downforce and vehicle stability.

Vehicle Dynamics

Suspension geometry, brake modeling, steering design, and simulation-driven optimization to maximize handling, stability, and driver feedback.

Electronics & Testing

Custom sensor integration, data acquisition, wiring harness design, and on-track validation to improve reliability and performance metrics.
