Who We Are

Ashwa Racing is a Formula Student combustion team from RV College of Engineering, dedicated to designing, engineering, and manufacturing high-performance formula-style race cars. With over two decades of innovation, Ashwa remains one of India’s leading collegiate motorsports teams.

Learn More

Engineering Focus

Electrical & Testing

Development and integration of electrical systems for EV and CV platforms, including testing, diagnostics, sensors, and vehicle validation.

Suspension & Administration

Design of kinematics, geometry, and compliance systems alongside team administration, documentation, and operational coordination.

Chassis & Workshop Management

Structural frame design, manufacturing supervision, and workshop resource management ensuring quality and safety in fabrication.

Aerodynamics

End-to-end aerodynamic development including CFD-driven wing sets, cooling ducts, underbody flow, and drag–downforce optimization.

Brakes & Logistics

Brake system modeling, calibration, and test planning combined with transport, inventory, and event logistics for competition readiness.

IT Systems

Software tools, website development, telemetry processing, and digital automation supporting team operations and engineering.

Sponsorship & Marketing

Corporate outreach, branding, social media management, and partner relations strengthening the team's industrial presence.

Finance

Budgeting, procurement planning, cost optimization, and resource allocation ensuring efficient team operations.
